Simple Deviled Eggs

The delicious-looking, mouth-watering deviled eggs.

Everyone needs an egg or two from the devil! (or at least that's what I think the name comes from). Best eaten with one whole egg a serving.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 12 egg halves to be exact. So you'll need 6 boiled eggs.
  • Mayonnaise: it makes the filling extra creamy.
  • Sugar: a teaspoon is enough for a little sweetness.
  • Vinegar: a teaspoon of white vinegar to battle the sweetness with acidity (yuck).
  • Mustard
  • Vegetables: we only mean onions and celery.
  • Seasoning: use salt & peppa (I mean paprika, you can add pepper too cause it fits).

Steps

  1. Cut the boiled eggs in half and remove the yolks (that is the yellow part, dummy). Reserve the whites.
  2. Mash the yolks with a fork and add all the other ingredients.
  3. Stuff the eggs! With the filling I mean.

Congratulations. You just made deviled eggs by yourself with absolutely no help from mom or sis (I'm trying to believe it).
